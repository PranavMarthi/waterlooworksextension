Quick Start

  1. Open WaterlooWorks Full Cycle or Employer-Student job postings.
  2. Open any posting once so the extension can detect your folder list.
  3. Click the extension popup, press Refresh, and pick your default shortlist folder.
  4. Use Left/Right arrows to move between postings.
  5. Use Up to shortlist or remove a posting from your shortlist folder.
